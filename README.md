Crime City is built to be a secure online platform for reporting crimes anonymously or publicly. It stores incidents in a database &amp; connects users with emergency services: police, fire, ambulance, legal aid. Designed to empower communities by making crime reporting accessible &amp; safe, considering the crime rate in Bangladesh &amp; crime against women.


### ABOUT CRIME CITY
In many situations, people struggle to report crimes due to fear, lack of access, or uncertainty about where to seek help. Victims or witnesses may be physically unable to reach a police station or feel unsafe doing so. This website is designed to eliminate those barriers by offering a secure platform where anyone can report crimes—openly or anonymously—whether they are victims, witnesses, or concerned citizens.

Beyond reporting, the site connects users with essential emergency contacts, including the police, fire brigade, ambulance, and legal aid. By making crime reporting and emergency help more accessible and immediate, this platform empowers individuals to take action without fear, delays, or confusion. Developed as a database management system project, this initiative carries the vision of becoming a real-world solution for safer, more responsive communities.
